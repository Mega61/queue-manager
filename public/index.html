Solace Queue Consumer

idle

Without Auto-ACK, messages are read but not removed from the queue (the broker redelivers them when the session ends). Enable it only when this consumer should consume the queue exclusively. Bodies are also saved under received/.

Publish

Uses the same broker/VPN/user/password above. Persistent messages wait for a broker acknowledgement; direct messages are fire-and-forget. Topic publishes reach any subscribed queue or direct subscriber; queue publishes go straight to that durable queue.
